Tirunelveli District Facts: This is the second largest district in Tamil Nadu after Villupuram District. There are 27 railway stations in this district. It is one of the major producers of wind energy in Tamil Nadu. It houses the famous Koodankulam Nuclear Power Plant.  

 

Major Agricultural Products: Chillies, coconut, gingelly, pulses, groundnut, ragi, cumbu, indigo, cotton, rice, bananas, spices

 

Major Industries: Textile, food, forestry

 

What is Tirunelveli District Famous For: Rich in mineral resources like limestone, sulphides and ilmenite-garnet sands
